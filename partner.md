## Partner with EthStaker

EthStaker is a 501(c)(4) nonprofit committed to remaining neutral for the thousands of stakers that depend on the resources we support.

Many groups have asked how they can work with us. This guide outlines options and guardrails for partnering with and supporting EthStaker’s mission to support the health and decentralization of the Ethereum staking ecosystem.

If you have ideas not outlined here, contact <a href="mailto:team@ethstaker.org">team@ethstaker.org</a> to discuss. Read more about EthStaker and the mission at [ethstaker.org/about](/about).



#### Marketplace

- We curate and share lists of tools and software that stakers rely on in their production stack. Any tool we feature must meet our bar for open source, security, decentralization, and overall community trust.
- For tools that meet that bar, paid promotion is available to boost visibility. This is not pay-for-play: payment can raise the prominence of qualifying tools, but it cannot buy inclusion for anything that doesn't already meet our standards.


#### Sponsor a specific thing

- We offer the opportunity to sponsor specific things such as collectibles, supporting surveys, or other such ideas.
- Sponsoring does not buy influence or control over the content of a given thing.


#### Event sponsor

- For events like Staking Gathering and other things EthStaker organizes, sponsors can support the event, have a booth, sponsor the Wifi / coffee, etc. More details are outlined in the specific sponsor guides for each event.
- Example: Staking Gathering at Devconnect Buenos Aires 2025 [sponsor guide](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1BkxUykbg_QVEcKh8y6alqoy2uukQF1Ashl5nK8Yfzus/edit?slide=id.g32f50f069ff_0_172#slide=id.g32f50f069ff_0_172)


#### Donate

- We allow for donations in custom amounts or frequency toward our mission of supporting the health and decentralization of the Ethereum staking ecosystem.
- EthStaker is a 501(c)(4) nonprofit, so contributions are generally not tax-deductible as charitable donations. Please consult your tax advisor about your specific situation.
- Donations signal support. They do not buy influence or anything of value. There is no token. EthStaker is independent and stewarded by the community.
- More info: [ethstaker.org/donate](/donate)

Contact us to discuss opportunities: <a href="mailto:team@ethstaker.org">team@ethstaker.org</a>
