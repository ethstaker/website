EthStaker is a 501(c)(4) nonprofit organization that operates only with the support of individuals, companies, and grants from the community we serve. We are grateful to those that give toward the ongoing mission of supporting the health and decentralization of the Ethereum staking ecosystem.

If you or your business have benefitted from EthStaker resources, education, events, value independent staker tools, or otherwise want to support our mission to keep Ethereum decentralized, please consider a donation.

Donors can choose to be featured on a public page on our website acknowledging that support or remain anonymous.
